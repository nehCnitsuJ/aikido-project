Since 2011, aikiForest has been the market leader in providing Aikido enrichment programme to kindergartens and child care centers . All our teaching staff undergo rigorous teacher training and have to pass medical checks stipulated by Early Childhood Development Agency (ECDA) before they are being deployed out.

We also have experienced teaching staff providing enrichment programme to schools in Johor Bahru.

Your school or centre can choose to engage our services through the following:

1. Core Enrichment Programme (within curriculum time).
2. Optional Enrichment Programme (opt in by parents outside curriculum time).

To find out more contact us at schools@aikiForest.com For our programmes at Johor Bahru contact us at jb@aikiForest.com

And it can be conducted at either our main outlets or at your premises.
