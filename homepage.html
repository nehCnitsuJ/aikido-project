Aikido is a Japanese self-defense martial arts which is often translated as "the way of harmonious spirit". The primary goal of aikido is to overcome oneself instead of cultivating violence and aggressivess, said by the founder of Aikido - Ueshiba Morihei. This involves throwing and twisting techniques which aims in turning the attacker's momentum against himself. Through Aikido, one will undergo the learning curve of maintaining a mental calm and control of one's body.
