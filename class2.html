Children Class

Children Class

At aikiForest, we believe that every child is unique. Classes are deliberately kept small with instructor-to-student ratio of 1:7 to ensure quality classroom interaction.

Through our colourfully illustrated Big Books, we tell simple yet interesting stories to the young ones. Each story reveals a fundamental Aikido movement or technique. Our creative & age-appropriate curriculum & pedagogy is simply unrivalled, with a range of teaching materials that few martial arts schools can match.

We make learning easy & fun.

Youth & Adult Class

Youth & Adult Class

Are you looking for something that combines ballet, judo and meditation?

Aikido consists of set movements that are graceful and train you to have good posture and balance. It is fun, physically challenging and has deceptively simple throws and locks that require intuition understanding of body mechanic to execute well. Aikido is often described as moving meditation too, where aikido is to be appreciated beyond the physical level and become mindfulness in motion.

  • Age 16 & above

  • Fellowship-based

  • Focus on Ukemi & Dynamic Body Movement

  • Appreciation of Body Mechanics

  • Real life Application of Aikido
