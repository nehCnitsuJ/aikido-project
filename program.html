Preschool Seed Enrichment Aikido Enrichment for Kindergartens and Child Care Centres.

Preschool Seed Enrichment Programme

Age 4-6 (N2-K2 Level)

This programme partake in a term thematic approach which focuses on learning fundamentals through activities & games. It also includes home-based revision videos, worksheets to reinforce understanding as well as assessment & upgrading to reflect child's progress. This programme can also cater for integrating classroom showcase or school performance.

Since 2011, aikiForest has been the market leader in providing Aikido enrichment programme to kindergartens and child care centers . All our teaching staff undergo rigorous teacher training and have to pass medical checks stipulated by Early Childhood Development Agency (ECDA) before they are being deployed out.

We also have experienced teaching staff providing enrichment programme to schools in Johor Bahru.

Your school or centre can choose to engage our services through the following:

1. Core Enrichment Programme (within curriculum time).
2. Optional Enrichment Programme (opt in by parents outside curriculum time).

Activity & Excursion Aikido Activity for Primary Schools, Student Care Centres & Others.

Scratching your head to search for providers of holiday activities? Looking for a suitable excursion venue? Proposing a new CCA or PAL? Fret not. We have the solution for you.

A customised Aikido-related activity can be worked out together with you, be it a kindergarten, child care centre, primary school or student care centre.

And it can be conducted at either our main outlets or at your premises.

Here are some examples of what we provide:

PAL: Programme for Active Learning

Age 7-8 (P1-P2 Level)

This programme consists of 7-10 modules of sports & games. You can select from 3 different domains: Mind Cultivation, Physical Conditioning or Life Skills. The main objective of this programme is to focus on social emotional learning.

Age 7-12 (P1-P6 Level)

This programme is conducted as a regular on-going class where students will be graded as they aim towards obtaining different coloured belts. The main objective of this focuses on the acquisition of basic techniques
