Welcome To aikiForest

The educational powerhouse of contemporary aikido

We deliver & develop high-quality & learner-centric aikido programmes. Catering towards all age groups starting from as young as 4 years old and teaching them how to protect themselves using aikido.

Aikido is a Japanese self-defense martial arts which is often translated as "the way of harmonious spirit". The primary goal of aikido is to overcome oneself instead of cultivating violence and aggressivess, said by the founder of Aikido - Ueshiba Morihei. This involves throwing and twisting techniques which aims in turning the attacker's momentum against himself. At aikiForest, we offer various of classes ranging from children to youth & adult classes. Students will be able to learn movement & technique fundamentals from basic to advance levels based on their age groups.
